So let’s take a tour of what features we have:

Easy starting

Register your company’s profile in few steps or import your company’s data from:

  • Linked In
  • Xing

Company page

Make your company page to be more attractive for heroes. Get the general data about you, photos and video, advantages, your brances etc.

More informative resume’s

It’s more informative, more open, more social. Look not only for employees, but also for personalities with their own stories to tell.

Dashboard

Easy to use and intuitive dashboard let’s you easily manage your account, search for heroes, make job offers, chat with heroes, receive notifications etc.

Searching & posting job offers

You can post job offers and target them as well as search heroes by interested criteria. Make searching folders and add heroes to them, mark them as favorites, purchase contacts, chat with heroes & make them an offer they can’t refuse.

Matching, KI score & AI plans

It sounds so Futurama-like, but we’re working every day on improving our searching algorithm. We want to give you results that fits you best like search engines do, analyzing lot’s of seen and hidden criteria. Also we’re planning to add AI in search engine in futue to make it more awesome.

User score

We give an internal score to each user. All information about the person and online statistics (access to profiles, number of contact purchases, etc.) are included in the score calculation.

The exact factors that influence the score will not be published. It is comparable to the Google principle the more appropriate results we deliver to companies the more valuable the service is
